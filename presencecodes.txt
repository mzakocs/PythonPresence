Different codes returned by this system per extension:
    available: the extension is online and not currently in a call
    talk <user>: the extension is currently in a call with <user>
    call <user>: the extension has sent a call to <user> (outbound)
    ring <user>: the extension is being called by <user> (inbound)
    unregistered: the extension currently has no registered devices and is offline
